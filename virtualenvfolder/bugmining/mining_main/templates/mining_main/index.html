Classifier Guide:
Enter Release Changelog in the form of [Previous Release]..[Next Release],
e.g: 66.0.3359.181..67.0.3396.62

Enter metrics in the form of [metric 1], [metrics 2], [metric 3]
e.g: 1,2,3, where
  • 0 = Number of lines ADDED
  • 1 = Number of lines DELETED
  • 2 = Average of lines ADDED
  • 3 = Average of lines DELETED
  • 4 = Average of CHANGESET
